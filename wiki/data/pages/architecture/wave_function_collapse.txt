====== Architecture: Wave Function Collapse ======

The Tartsi generator uses a wave-function-collapse-inspired process.

===== In plain language =====

Each cell in each ring begins with several possible symbols.
The solver picks one cell, chooses a symbol, and then removes incompatible symbols from neighboring cells.
This repeats until the whole circle is resolved.

===== Why it works well here =====

  * Magic circles are repetitive but not identical.
  * Neighbor relationships matter visually.
  * Deterministic seeds are easy to understand and test.
